How to Get to Mabini

🚗

By Car

2-3 hours via STAR Tollway → Batangas City → Mabini

🚌

By Bus

Manila → Batangas City → Jeepney to Anilao/Mabini

🏍️

Local Transport

Tricycles to resorts; boats for island hopping
